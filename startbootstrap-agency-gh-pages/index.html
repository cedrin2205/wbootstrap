The Classic Mirai Tank

Reliable and timeless rainwater storage

Durable Construction: Rugged stainless-steel design for long-lasting performance.
Timeless Design: Bold red funnel top with an industrial-inspired look.
Smart Monitoring System: Tracks water levels and alerts for maintenance.
Versatile Application: Ideal for residential, agricultural, and commercial use.
Weather-Resistant: Engineered to withstand the elements for reliable, year-round use.

The Compact Mirai Tank

Space-saving and efficient rainwater storage

Compact Design: Sleek, cylindrical shape with a tapered top for a discreet, space-saving fit.
Impressive Storage Capacity: Holds up to 1,500 gallons of rainwater.
Durable Construction: Made from high-quality stainless steel for long-lasting reliability.
Smart Monitoring System: Tracks water levels and provides maintenance alerts.

Weather-Resistant: Built to withstand the elements for dependable year-round performance.

Comprehensive Monitoring: Tracks water levels, quality, and system health with insights and alerts.
Sustainable Solution: Optimized for efficient and eco-friendly rainwater management.
